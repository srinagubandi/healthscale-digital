About Us

At Health Scale Digital, we specialize in performance-based marketing solutions tailored for the healthcare industry. Our Cost-per-Outcome™ (CPO) model ensures you pay only for tangible results that align with your specific goals, such as patient appointments, service inquiries, or telehealth sign-ups.

Our Approach

Cost-per-Outcome™ (CPO)

Our unique CPO model means you invest only in actual, measurable outcomes that matter to your healthcare organization. Whether it's new patient registrations, appointment bookings, or service inquiries, we align our success with yours.

Science Based Acquisition

We use machine learning, AI, advanced analytics, and our healthcare expertise to improve key performance indicators, whether for patient acquisition or filling your B2B sales funnel. We work on a performance basis, ensuring we are invested in our shared success.
